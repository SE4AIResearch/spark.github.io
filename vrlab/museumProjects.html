2. Virtual Museum for Animal World

3. Virtual Reality Manufacturing Museum as a Tool for Learning and Navigation
The ability of Virtual Reality (VR) to explain the principles of design, manufacturing and business far surpasses that of books. Virtual environments (VE) provide facility for experiential learning by allowing the students to explore the VE at their own pace and interact in real time. Game creation is used as a metaphor for early design education. The VRML Museum project was conceived with the interest of informing the public about the evolution of current manufacturing systems. The primary objective of this project is to create a museum exhibit that will educate and increase awareness of the progress of manufacturing. The project’s goals are to educate participants about manufacturing, expose them to the computer/technology advancements that have occurred.
