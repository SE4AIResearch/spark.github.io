CRT OUTPUT DEVICES


  • CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) Device

    • A rapidly moving electronic beam creates the picture on the surface of the CRT (screen, monitor, TV set).


  • Random Scan CRT

    • - also called Vector Display or Stroke Device
    - beam moves around 'randomly' (controlled by a program)
    - operates in storage or refresh mode
    - outdated technology, marked beginning of computer graphics (Tektronix storage tube)


  • Raster Scan CRT

    • - also called Raster Display or Raster Scan Device
    - beam moves always in the same pattern (line by line)
    - along each line, a fixed number of pixels (dots) are turned on or off
    - operates always in refresh mode
    - similar to TV technology
    - all current display technology (including LCD) is based on Raster Scan principle
