COLOR INTERPOLATION


  • Color Interpolation


    • - creating a continuously changing sequence of colors between given key colors

    - used for:
      color coding: temperature distribution in a weather map
      shading: changing light reflection across a surface (Gouraud shading)
      animation: Color Interpolator node in VRML (color change in time)
      others: antialiasing, fading (from one image to the next), ....
