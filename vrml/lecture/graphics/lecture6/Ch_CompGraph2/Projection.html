PROJECTION SYSTEMS


  • Human Eye


    • Light rays enter the eye via the crystalline lens.
    Images are projected up-side-down on the curved retina.



  • Pinhole Camera


    • Most simple camera design, lens system is replaced by a small hole.
    All incoming light rays intersect at the hole.

    Images are projected up-side-down on the film plane.

    Large/far objects and small/near objects may have the same projected size (perspective foreshortening).

    If captured image is presented properly to eye, rays from image seem to come from same location as rays from object.
