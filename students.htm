Research Assistant Positions are available for both Graduate and Undergraduate students in Virtual Reality Laboratory (VR Lab). The students are expected to work on various research projects in Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Multi-User Gaming, Agent based Modeling, 3D Animation, Data Science, Data Visualization, and Mobile App development. The students would be expected to work in the VR Laboratory (VR Hardware Lab and VR Software Lab). The current list of projects in the VR lab can be found at the VR Lab website. Students will be working on Gaming Engine: Unity 3D (uses C#, Java) and Vizard (uses Python). Students who have taken COSC 477 (Virtual Reality and its Principles), COSC 729 (Virtual Reality and its Applications), COSC 829 (Advanced Virtual Reality Systems) or are proficient in Unity 3D, C#, Java will be preferred.
  • Graduate students would be paid at an hourly rate of $15/hr-$17/hr for 20 hrs/week during regular semesters and maximum of 40 hrs/week in summer.
  • Undergraduate students would be paid at an hourly rate of $12/hr -$14/hr for 20 hrs/week during regular semesters and maximum of 40 hrs/week in summer.
