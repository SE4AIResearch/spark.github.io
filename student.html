Students

Research Assistant Positions are available

Research Assistant Positions are available for both Graduate and Undergraduate students. The students are expected to work on various research projects in Data Science, Data Visualization, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Multi-User Gaming, Agent based Modeling, 3D Animation, and Mobile App development. The current list of projects in the can be found at the DVXR Lab website. Students will be working on Gaming Engine: Unity 3D (uses C#, Java) and Vizard (uses Python). Students who are proficient in Unity 3D, C#, Java, Power BI, Tableau will be preferred.
